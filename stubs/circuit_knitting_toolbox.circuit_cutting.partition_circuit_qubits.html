circuit_knitting_toolbox.circuit_cutting.partition_circuit_qubits¶

partition_circuit_qubits(circuit, partition_labels)[source]¶

Replace all nonlocal gates belonging to more than one partition with instances of TwoQubitQPDGate.

TwoQubitQPDGates belonging to a single partition will not be affected.

Parameters:

  • circuit (QuantumCircuit) – The circuit to partition

  • partition_labels (Sequence[Hashable]) – A sequence containing a partition label for each qubit in the input circuit. Nonlocal gates belonging to more than one partition will be replaced with QPDGates.

Returns:

The output circuit with each nonlocal gate spanning

two partitions replaced by a TwoQubitQPDGate.

Return type:

(QuantumCircuit)

Raises:

  • ValueError – Length of partition_labels does not equal the number of qubits in the circuit

  • ValueError – Cannot decompose gate which acts on more than 2 qubits
